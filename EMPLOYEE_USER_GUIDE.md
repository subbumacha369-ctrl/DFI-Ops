# Operations OS — Employee User Guide

A quick guide for employees using Operations OS in the browser.

## Supported browsers & devices
| Platform | Browser | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Desktop | Google Chrome (latest) | ✅ Supported |
| Desktop | Microsoft Edge (latest) | ✅ Supported |
| Mobile | Android Chrome | ✅ Supported (responsive) |
| Mobile | iPhone Safari | ✅ Supported (responsive) |

The layout is responsive: on phones/tablets the sidebar collapses into a menu (tap the **☰** icon top-left);
cards, tables, boards, and forms reflow to a single column. Tasks, projects, dashboard, and forms are usable
on touch screens.

---

## First login
1. Open the app URL your administrator shared (e.g. `https://<your-domain>`).
2. You'll land on the **sign-in** page. Enter the **email** you were invited with and your **password**.
   - If you signed up yourself, check your inbox for a **confirmation email** first.
   - You can also use **Continue with Google** if your admin enabled it.
3. If two-factor (MFA) is required, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
4. You'll arrive at your organization **Dashboard**.

## Change password
- **Forgot it:** on the sign-in page click **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and follow the reset link.
- **Set a new one:** open the link → enter and confirm a strong password (8+ chars, upper/lower/number).
- Profile details (name, phone) can be updated from **Settings**.

## Join an organization
- You join by **invitation**. When an admin invites you, you receive an email with an **Accept invitation**
  link. Click it, sign in (or create your account), and you'll be added to the organization.
- If you opened the app without an org, you'll be prompted to **create an organization** or accept a pending invite.

## View tasks
1. In the sidebar, open your workspace section and click **Tasks**.
2. Switch views with the tabs:
   - **List** — all your tasks with status, priority, due date, assignee.
   - **Kanban** — tasks grouped by status column.
   - **Calendar** — tasks placed on their due dates.
3. Click any task to open its details. (Managers can also reach your tasks from the **Dashboard** → click a
   KPI card or your name in the **Workload** chart to drill into the list.)

## Update tasks
1. Click a task to open it.
2. Change **Status** (Created → Accepted → In Progress → On Hold → Completed → Verified → Closed),
   **Priority**, **Assignee**, or **Due date** using the controls at the top.
3. Add **subtasks**, **comments** (type `@name` style by selecting a teammate to notify them), or
   **attachments** (Attach file).
4. On a **Kanban** board you can **drag a card** between columns to change its status.

## Create tasks
1. Go to **Tasks** and click **+ New task**.
2. Enter a **title**, optional description, **priority**, **due date**, **assignee**, and **project**.
3. Click **Create task**. The assignee is notified automatically.
4. Recurring work: open the task and set a **Recurrence** (daily/weekly/monthly) — a new occurrence is created
   when you complete it.

## Project usage
1. Sidebar → **Projects**. Click a project to open it.
2. The project page shows **Progress %**, **Milestones** (tap to mark complete), the **Team**, and the
   project's **task board**.
3. To create a project (if permitted): **Projects → + New project**, then add milestones, members, and tasks.

## Dashboard usage
1. The **Dashboard** shows your organization's health: Total / Completed / Pending / Overdue tasks, Active /
   Delayed projects, completion rate, trend, workload, and priority breakdown.
2. **Everything is clickable** — click any KPI card or chart to **drill down** into the underlying list
   (e.g., click **Overdue** to see exactly which tasks are overdue, then click a row to open it).
3. Use the **filter bar** (date range, department, team, project, employee) to focus the numbers.
4. What you see depends on your **role** — some widgets or menu items may be hidden by your administrator.

## Getting help
- The **bell** (top-right) shows your notifications (assignments, mentions, comments, deadlines).
- Use the **theme** toggle for light/dark mode.
- For access or permission issues, contact your organization administrator.
